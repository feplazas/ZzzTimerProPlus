Privacy Policy

Zzz Timer Pro+
Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

Zzz Timer Pro+ ("the App") is developed by Felipe Plazas. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our sleep timer application.

We value your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

We are committed to transparency. Here's what you need to know:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services.
  • No Advertising: The app does not contain ads and does not share data with advertisers.
  • No Network Requests: The app functions entirely offline.

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Timer Settings: Your preferred timer duration and sound preferences.
  • Sleep Statistics: Session history and usage data for your personal reference.
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, vibration preferences, and other customizations.
  • CPAP Reminder Settings: If enabled, your CPAP maintenance reminder preferences.

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when the timer completes.
  • Do Not Disturb: Optional - to enable quiet mode during sleep sessions.
  • Microphone (Sleep Tracking): Optional - used locally for sleep sound monitoring, never recorded or transmitted.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback when timers complete.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
